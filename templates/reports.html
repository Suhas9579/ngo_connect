NGO Data Exports & Reports

Extract audit sheets, donation ledgers, and attendance rosters in PDF and CSV format.

Tracks inventories allocated to active events, quantity changes, and lists quantities used vs. quantities remaining.

6. Monthly NGO Activity

A consolidated overview of the NGO's performance for the selected month, combining total new volunteers, donation totals, and events closed.
